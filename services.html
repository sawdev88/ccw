Colorado Custom Wraps can apply Plasti-Dip to your vehicle in any color whether it is a satin, matte, gloss, pearl or flake finish. We have it all. The pearl changes colors according to the way the light is hitting on your vehicle. Today Plasti-Dip has improved dramatically over the last year! Your friends will never know it's a rubber paint when they see it.

If you like the way your vehicle is, we can always apply a clear coat on it to protect the paint. If you like the color of your vehicle but want a satin, matte or flat finish Colorado Custom Wraps can do that too! The clear coat along with any other paint we use can peel off when you want your car back to it's original finish.

Imagine if you had a newer vehicle and you could put a clear bra over the entire car to protect it for under $600, why wouldn't you?? The possibilities are endless!

3M VINYL SELECTION

Are you wanting to have a different color on your vehicle? Or do you have a work vehicle for your business that you want to wrap with your business name and graphics to? If so Colorado Custom Wraps is the place for you. We do it all!

Colorado Custom Wraps uses 3m Scotchprint Wrap Film that will help you make your car stand out in a distinctive way. 3.5mil dual cast film provides just the right amount of rigidity. Slide able and repositionable pressure activated adhesive with non-visible air release channels for virtually no air bubbles and the application is fast and easy so you will not have to be without a vehicle for weeks.

We can do cars, trucks, vans, shuttle bus and fleet buses, trailers, motorcycles, business vehicles and more!

Info & Gallery

Hydrographics or HydroGraphics, also known as immersion printing, water transfer printing, water transfer imaging, hydro dipping or cubic printing, is a method of applying printed designs to three-dimensional surfaces. The hydrographic process can be used on metal, plastic, glass, hard woods, and various other material.
